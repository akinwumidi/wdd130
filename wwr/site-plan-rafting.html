Author: Israel Akinwumi

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of establishing an online presence for our rafting business is to enhance accessibility, reach a broader audience, and streamline customer interactions. By creating a dynamic website, we aim to provide a platform where adventure seekers can easily discover our rafting services, learn about our offerings, and conveniently make reservations. This online presence will serve as a central hub for information, fostering engagement, and creating a seamless experience for both new and returning customers.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website includes adventure enthusiasts, thrill-seekers, and nature lovers seeking an adrenaline-filled experience. The website is designed to cater to individuals aged 18-45, who are physically active and enjoy outdoor activities.
